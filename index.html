Tic-Tac-Toe

also known as X and O, is a classic two-player game where players take turns marking spaces in a 3x3 grid with their respective symbols, X or O. The objective of the game is to be the first player to get three of their symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The game begins with an empty grid, The first player places their symbol in an empty square, followed by the second player placing their symbol in an empty square. This alternation continues until one player achieves three consecutive symbols in a line, winning the game, or until all squares are filled, resulting in a draw if no player has won.
